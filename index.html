What is SOVEREIGN? We are a Large Crypto Family

Sovereign is our base platform for future crypto innovation, a currency that will have real world use. We believe that crypto is a family. We have holders who know we will make great things, and those that just mine for profit. Both are what makes this family great.

So why choose Sovereign? Because you too can be part of this great family, we turn no one away - even those spreading FUD, because you are still part of the greater Crypto Family.

 

DISCOVERWhy us?

We have a solid team consisting of some of the original SOV team, The HTH Team, and The Hash-Kings Team. We are a dedicated group and plan to bring many innovations to crypto over the coming years and we want you to be part of this future. We all fully believe in crypto and that it is the future of currency. This drives us to build on the crypto platform and make it great.
